We are

Akker Expo-Impo

Having its origin in Kenya AKKER EXPO-IMPO prides itself as a social enterprise that seeks to connect poor out-growers to markets in Europe and the Middle East and other parts of the world.The founders of the company have worked in the civil society of Kenya and desired to connect the poor to markets as a way of the changing their poverty situations for good.Our farm acts as a model to these out-growers providing them with the exposure and skills that they require.We source our products from poor young women and men growing safe and traceable fruits.We specialize in growth and export of avocados.By buying products from our company,you contribute in changing the lives of poor women and men in the rural areas of of Kenya ,hence solving the unemployment menace facing Kenya today.
